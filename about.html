Our story

Founded to deliver,
not just plan

Martone Planning Studio was founded in 2011 under Martone Investments (Pty) Ltd, to help public and private clients turn complex land, infrastructure and development briefs into clear, deliverable plans.

We combine statutory planning knowledge, spatial design thinking and project coordination with close attention to local context, producing plans that are technically sound and realistic to deliver.

FAQ

Common questions

What services does Martone offer?

Town planning, urban design, master planning, project management, development consultancy, retail network planning and socio-economic analysis.

What kind of clients do you work with?

We work with both public bodies and private clients, including developers, landowners, investors and retail or fuel-network operators who need land-use, subdivision, rezoning or site-planning support.

What does your process typically look like?

Engagements usually begin with an initial consultation to understand the site and the brief, followed by feasibility and spatial analysis, statutory submission and approval, and coordination through to delivery.

How do I start a project with you?

Get in touch via the contact page — we will arrange an initial consultation to understand your site and goals and outline next steps.
