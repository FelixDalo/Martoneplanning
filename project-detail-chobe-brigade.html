Overview

Master plan for a public infrastructure site in the Chobe Region

Chobe Brigade was one of the project master plans designed and approved by Martone Planning Studio in collaboration with HSNV Group, under the Development Manager Model Package No. 1. The package was introduced in 2023 as a procurement strategy to accelerate infrastructure delivery under the Second Transitional National Development Plan (2023–2025).

Martone's role was to design a master plan for the site and carry it through to statutory approval — contributing to a coordinated programme of public infrastructure projects across the Chobe Region, all delivered under the same package and timeline.
